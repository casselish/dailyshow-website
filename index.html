The Daily Show!

The Daily Show has been running on Comedy Central since 1996. Its hosts have included Craig Kilborn, Jon Stewart, and, most recently, Trevor Noah. Under Jon Stewart, the show revolutionized the ways in which satire and political comedy were presented to television audiences.

"The Daily Show: The Book" is a comprehensive oral history of the show since its inception, and a very useful resource.

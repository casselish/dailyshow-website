Title

The Daily Show before Jon Stewart

Date

November 4, 2020

Time

5:45pm

Content

Before Jon Stewart took over in 199, the show was hosted by Craig Kilborn. In that era, the show focused much more on 'easier' targets, joking about celebrities at their lowest points and people with eccentric hobbies. Even lacking the later edge that Stewart would bring to it, the show was a success -- TV Guide named it the best in late night comedy, and Kilborn was nominated for a number of hosting awards.

However, Kilborn left unexpectedly in 1998, to take a position at The Late Late Show on CBS. As to be expected, there was a lot of uncertainty -- both within the network and within the show’s audience -- over who could replace him. In 1999, Jon Stewart -- a relatively unsuccessful comedian -- was hired, and the rest is history.
